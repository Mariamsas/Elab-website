The Problem

Despite rapid growth, Rwanda's creative sector faces challenges: lack of support, marginalization, and exploitation. Young, talented individuals in urban areas struggle with limited funding, few places to create or showcase their work, and high unemployment. Creative careers are undervalued compared to STEM, and even high-quality artistic products suffer from poor marketing and distribution.

Our Solution

We envision a platform that empowers young artists by providing opportunities to showcase their work, access resources, and receive mentorship. By fostering a creative environment and offering training, we aim to build a sustainable, diversified economy and improve life prospects for Rwanda's youth.
