Our Services: Creating Jobs & Opportunities

Training

Providing workshops and mentorship to help young creatives develop their skills and prepare for careers in the arts, design, and culture sectors. We offer both online and in-person courses to make learning accessible for everyone.

Sell Art

Offering a platform for artists to showcase and sell their work, connecting them with buyers and new markets to generate income and recognition.

Event

Organizing exhibitions, performances, and cultural events that provide exposure, networking, and job opportunities for young talent.

Partnership

Building collaborations with organizations, businesses, and mentors to create more opportunities and resources for youth in the creative industry.

The Problem

Despite rapid growth, Rwanda's creative sector faces challenges: lack of support, marginalization, and exploitation. Young, talented individuals in urban areas struggle with limited funding, few places to create or showcase their work, and high unemployment. Creative careers are undervalued compared to STEM, and even high-quality artistic products suffer from poor marketing and distribution.

Our Solution

We envision a platform that empowers young artists by providing opportunities to showcase their work, access resources, and receive mentorship. By fostering a creative environment and offering training, we aim to build a sustainable, diversified economy and improve life prospects for Rwanda's youth.
